# Product-architecture human gates

Stop before issue creation or execution when the work would decide product taste, role or personality behavior, production or release state, auth or session policy, billing or entitlements, privacy or legal commitments, credentials or private data, destructive migration, external publication, or launch readiness.

Also stop when the evidence falls outside the permitted scope, the issue list cannot be bounded and dependency-ordered, repository freshness is unsafe, or a high score leaves only subjective polish.

The stop packet names the exact decision needed, evidence that triggered the gate, viable options and tradeoffs, risk if deferred, and the downstream route after a human decision. It never hides uncertainty behind a score or silently creates executable scope.
