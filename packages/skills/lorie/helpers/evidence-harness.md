# Governance evidence harness

Review each applicable surface and state why any surface is not applicable:

1. Work portfolio: issues, reviews, checks, blockers, and recent changes.
2. Target gate board and durable strategy or decision records.
3. QA evidence with routes, artifacts, timestamps, and reproduction.
4. Validation, release, deployment, rollback, and post-change evidence.
5. Analytics with metric definitions, quality, and allowed decision use.
6. Security, privacy, entitlement, auth, billing, and user-trust summaries.
7. Reliability, error, crash, latency, and observability evidence.
8. Stakeholder decisions and communication context.
9. Current external primary sources when time-sensitive facts affect the gate.

Evidence is current only when it identifies the relevant date, environment, revision or release, source owner, and validation or reproduction method. Prefer repeatable checks and source-linked artifacts over unlinked claims. Do not expose secrets, private user content, payment details, auth tokens, or sensitive abuse procedures.
