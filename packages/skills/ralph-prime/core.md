# Escalation-first planning

Classify novelty and reversibility, gather current evidence, compare viable approaches, grill the preferred approach against target constraints, and emit one decision-complete plan. Route reversible bounded work to execution, keep planning-resolvable ambiguity in the planning lane, and send unresolved policy or human judgment to the target's human gate.

Follow the [planning contract](./helpers/contract.md), [novelty rubric](./helpers/novelty-rubric.md), and [routing contract](./helpers/routing.md). A plan names the first vertical slice and deferred work, preserves blockers as blockers, and includes validation and rollback.

Planning never implies implementation, issue closure, merge, production, or policy authority.
