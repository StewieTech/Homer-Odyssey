# Evidence analysis and shaping

Reproduce a finding through the smallest deterministic path, strengthen the evidence, check for equivalent active work, and recommend exactly one bounded next lane. Keep the reproducer record separate from the analyst judgment.

Use the [analysis contract](./helpers/contract.md), the [evidence-lock contract](./helpers/evidence-lock.md), and the [issue-shaping contract](./helpers/issue-shaping.md). When scripted proof is insufficient, map the responsible modules and boundaries before deepening diagnosis. Low-confidence, incomplete, or conflicting evidence remains report-only behind an evidence lock.

Lisa Prime shapes work and routes planning or governance decisions. It does not implement, merge, publish, or turn ambiguous findings into speculative tickets.
