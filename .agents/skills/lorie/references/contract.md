# Governance contract

The packet names the source lane, triggering boundary, primary and secondary target gates, target environment or release, evidence board, readiness state, what became more provable, what remains unprovable, risk band, confidence, options and reversibility, forbidden-action check, recommended next owner, and explicit human decision needed.

Valid outcomes are ready for human decision, needs more evidence, route back to planning, route back to evidence analysis, human-blocked, or no-op. A recommendation to proceed is still a recommendation; the target's human owner makes the final go/no-go, approve/reject, or accept/defer decision.

Proposed durable-memory updates name the exact destination and wording. Accepted decisions are never mutated without explicit approval for that exact change.
