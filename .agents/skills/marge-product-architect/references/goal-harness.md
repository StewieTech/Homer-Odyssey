# Goal-harness contract

A goal harness defines what finished means for one product surface. It should name the desired outcome and non-goals, stable principles, weighted scoring dimensions, representative happy and recovery scenarios, route or module boundaries, allowed evidence, issue-shaping rules, human gates, and small examples.

Every dimension is scored from zero to ten with confidence and evidence references. A global score is allowed only when derived from the weights. Apply the lowest relevant evidence cap; incomplete runtime proof, inaccessible states, unknown privacy or accessibility behavior, or non-actionable handoff limits confidence and score.

If a broad or strategic target has no accepted harness, do not invent product direction inside the evaluation. Route to durable product shaping, deeper planning, or human judgment.
