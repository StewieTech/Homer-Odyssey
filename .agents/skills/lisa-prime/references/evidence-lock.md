# Evidence-lock contract

Evidence lock is a hard stop, not a weak bug report. Use it when the claimed path cannot be proven through a deterministic reproduction and adequate artifacts.

A blocker packet records the route or surface, claim reference, one stop condition, missing screenshots/logs/reproduction steps, a content-minimized rationale, next owner, and next evidence action. Typical stop conditions include incomplete artifact sequences, non-deterministic reproduction, unresolved module ownership, insufficient environment access, or conflicting product paths.

The lock clears only after the missing evidence is collected and the smallest path repeats. Until then, no implementation-ready issue, capability escalation, or speculative root-cause claim is allowed.
